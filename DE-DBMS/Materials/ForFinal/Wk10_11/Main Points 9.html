Lesson 9 - Transaction Management
 
Life without problems

A transaction has the qualities of atomicity (all or none), consistency (transaction running alone preserves the consistency of the database), isolation (plays well with others) and durability (once complete, changes survive even system failures). Atomicity and durability are achieved by recovery mechanisms and isolation by concurrency control.

The qualities of the unified field include reverberating wholeness, all knowingness, integrating and harmonizing, invincible, and progressive. These qualities are enlivened by contact of the mind with the unified field of all the laws of nature during TM.

MAIN POINTS

1. If a schedule allows for the concurrent execution of two or more transactions, that schedule is serializable if the outcome is the same as it would be if the transactions were run serially (one after the other). In other words, the sequential behavior of a set of transactions defines the legal parallel behaviors of transactions. In nature the underlying sequential unfoldment of the unified field is at the basis of massive parallelism that is observed in nature.

2. A lock based protocol ensures serializability by requiring that access to data items be done in a mutually exclusive manner. This is the way that database integrity is maintained. In Nature, cultural integrity is maintained by “locking” certain values that are fundamental to a culture and not allowing them to be changed from outside.

3. To ensure the atomicity property of transactions, if a transaction fails, the database needs to recover from the transaction, i.e., undo any intermediate changes made by the transaction. This is essentially the removal of impurities from the database. In the same way, impurities (deep stresses) are removed from the physiology when very deep rest is gained during Transcendental Meditation.
