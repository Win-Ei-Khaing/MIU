Lesson 11 - Query Processing
 
Do Less and Accomplish More

A query processor is a program that translates a high level query language such as SQL into expressions that can be implemented at the physical level of the file system. It then performs optimizations on these expressions and executes them to retrieve data.

When acting in accord with natural law, all actions are spontaneously optimized to promote maximum evolution.

MAIN POINTS

1. A query execution plan is the output of query decomposition and is a sequence of primitive operations that can be used to intelligently evaluate a query. The whole of individual and cosmic life is fundamentally intelligent because it proceeds systematically, containing its own order and unfolding in an overall orderly way, unifying individual purposes in multipurpose flow.

2. Heuristics (i.e. good guesses) are used in the final stage of query decomposition.Heuristical approaches save time but do not always produce the optimal execution plan. A heuristic is something the intellect uses to help make decisions with incomplete information. However, in enlightenment one’s actions are spontaneously in accord with all the laws of Nature.

3.  The statistics maintained by the DBMS are limited and may not be up to date; hence the estimated cost may not be truly in accord with the actual cost of the query. Once again, incompleteness of information is not present in enlightenment – there is spontaneous right action, one has all possibilities guiding one’s activity.
